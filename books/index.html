About Us

UPTI started in 2020 by an administration under mr. Mugabi Isaac.

The institution works and assissted by Odyssey technologies based in Canada.
It is a branch of Renzok university in Victoria Canada based on developing technology and human livelihood as a whole.

We provide the best education in technology particularly software engineering and web developing.

We also build and strength the connection of God and people plus the entire community as a whole through our bible learning and fellowshipping.

Still, we provide business ideas and connection to the people in Uganda plus the outside country as a whole and all this is through our business learning

Vison

Our goal is to inspire the students to define their purpose of life,
mission and legacy while integrating technology.

We seek the evolution of each student through their program at the institution ,
which serves as a bridge that allows them to reach their
full potential and achieve their dreams.

Mission

To be a higher institution concerned about generating
technological development alternatives likely to be
sustained in order to lead to a more efficient
administration of the world village and its environment,
exerting human and community rights through diversity
with the ultimate goal of the satisfaction and
evolution of the world
